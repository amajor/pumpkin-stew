Pumpkin Stew

An annual tradition.

WHEN

We've pretty much decided that the first Saturday of November is the magic day every year.

Next magic day:

November 2, 2013
4:00pm - 9:00pm EST

WHAT

We've got a little bit of an earlier start time for those with kiddos that want to come but don't want to sacrifice bedtime. But really, it's always come-and-go as you please.

We'll have stew available the entire evening, even at 4pm. So if you want to come at 4 and eat right away, that's great. If you want to come later and see a pumpkin come out of the oven, that magic will probably happen sometime after 6pm.

We'll have a fire in the fire ring outside, so if the house is too full, wear some warm clothes so that you have the option to hangout outside.

Extra things to know:

  • We'll provide stew, bread, and maybe some mulled cider
  • If you feel inclined to bring something to share, bring your favorite fall food or beverage (fermented or otherwise)
  • Feel free to invite a friend to take along
  • Kids are definitely okay to take along

WHERE

At the Major Cannery.

By Major Cannery, I mean our house. We do a lot of canning. The title seemed fitting.
